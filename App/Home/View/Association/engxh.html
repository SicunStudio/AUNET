        Introduction of the English Association:
        Brief introduction of the English Association: it was established in 1989, it’s one of the earliest established students association in HUST. Linked to the union of associations and attached to the school of foreign languages.

        Structure of the English Association:
        The association takes the department of Organization as our core, the other 5 departments, the Department of liaison , HR, Department of Event, Department of skills and ability ,the department of publicity .Propaganda Department are cooperating with the ministry of Organization to carry out events.
        The department of Organization is the core of our association, in charge of our every plan of every event, risk management and organizing.
        The Department of liaison, also called the department of public relation, is in charge of the fund issue which is one of the two foundations of the development of our association, the Public Relations Department raises money for our every event.
        HR is in charge of the membership issue which is also one of the two foundations of the development of our association, HR has the responsibilities to supervise the group leaders, sending information to register members and financial examination and approval, etc.

        Department of Event is the department, in charge of the specific work of every event, handling the auditorium service and supervise the development of special interest groups.
        The department of skills and ability is a department which is separated from the Department of Event, The department of skills and ability is in charge of the routines such as English Corner and Movie House.
        The Department of publicity is in charge of the packaging of every event, promote every event and enlarge the influence.
